About Thiwi

Premier client - 20/04/2024

Welcome to Thiwi.ttt, your destination for unique and personalized tattoos. Founded by Thibault Sandier, passionate about body art for over a year, my mission is to transform your ideas into works of art.

Thiwi.ttt prides itself on listening to each client to create a unique tattoo that reflects their story, while emphasizing creativity and originality.

Thiwi.ttt stands out for its exemplary seriousness and precision, while valuing creativity and listening. Each tattoo is a patiently designed work to reflect the uniqueness of each client.
